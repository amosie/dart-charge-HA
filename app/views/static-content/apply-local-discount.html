{{The local residents' discount scheme is available to people who live in the Dartford and Thurrock council tax zones.

Accounts are registered for one year and must be renewed annually.

The scheme only applies to personal vehicle use and only one vehicle can be registered for each resident.

Existing DART-Tag account holders

Existing DART-Tag account holders should apply for a local residents' discount through Connect Plus.

Existing Dart Charge account holders

Existing Dart Charge account holders can apply for a local residents' discount from within their Dart Charge account.

Registering a new account for when Dart Charge goes live

Please be aware that your local residents' discount will not be activated until proof of residence and vehicle ownership have been confirmed. We will let you know when this has happened.

Until then, you’ll have a standard account. No accounts will activated until the Dart Charge scheme goes live.

You will need

  • Proof of residency; you can scan documents or post them to us
  • Proof of vehicle ownership at the registered address
  • Registration number for vehicles(s) you want to register
  • £10 or £20 payment to activate your account balance
  • Payment details for automatic top up and direct debit

Limited discount

Apply for a local residents' limited discount (£10 per year for 50 crossings then 20p per crossing).

Unlimited discount

Apply for a local residents' unlimited discount (£20 per year).
